    ABOUT BLAMBOT

    Blambot is a world-class collection of software created by award-winning comic book letterer and graphic designer, Nate Piekos. For over twenty years, Blambot has been serving the independent and professional world of graphic design and comic book publishing with typography uniquely tailored to both traditional print and digital media. Blambot's portfolio includes projects for virtually all the major comic book publishers, celebrated actors, artists, and musicians. Beyond comics, Blambot's design projects include work for movies, television, novels, merchandising, and even a sold-out national rock and roll tour.
